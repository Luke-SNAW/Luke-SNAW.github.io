## 10일 휴식 후 조깅

I have rested from jogging for 10 days. It's okay to have a hiatus for 1 or 2 weeks.  
There is a training theory that 6 weeks of training plus 1 or 2 weeks of hiatus yields almost the same gains as 8 weeks of continuous training.  
I don't know if the same effect applies to aerobic exercise, but the fact is I need a hiatus for mental reasons anyway.

오늘도 딱히 뛰어야겠다는 생각은 들지 않았지만 뛰러나감. 아마 어제 오늘 편히 쉬었기 때문일 듯.
